Our story

Omooye was founded by educators who believed that character is built through action, not just words. "Walking the walk" means every student engages in community projects, outdoor leadership, and collaborative problem-solving.

We remain intentionally small — 96 students across 6 grades — so that each voice is heard and each path is guided.

Core values

  • Authenticity – be real, be present
  • Service – learn by doing good
  • Curiosity – question everything
  • Community – walk together

"We don't just teach subjects — we teach how to turn thought into meaningful action. That's the Omooye difference." — Mr S.J Ogunremi, Principal
